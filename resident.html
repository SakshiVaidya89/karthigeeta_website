Dining Pavilion

The Dining Pavilion, an extension to an indoor cafeteria at K.J. Somaiya Institute in Mumbai, serves as a link between existing buildings. Situated amidst trees, its platform design seamlessly connects the structures under a translucent roof.

Multi-wall polycarbonate roofing, suspended from beams, creates a continuous plane above, casting shadows from foliage below.
